The Best Movies According to Abhijit

My top 3 movies of all times.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending thriller by Christopher Nolan that explores the depths of dreams and reality, offering a unique narrative and stunning visuals.

Forrest Gump (1994)

A heartwarming tale of an extraordinary man, played by Tom Hanks, whose simple innocence and resilience touch on historical events and human connections.

Drishyam (2015)

A riveting thriller about a man who goes to great lengths to protect his family after they become involved in a crime.
